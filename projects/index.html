    SQL weekly challenges

    In this repository, I responded to the posed questions in some of the study cases of the course "8 week SQL challenge" by Danny Ma (Data with Danny). From main and basic SQL syntaxis to the elaboration of more complex structures, design of CTEs and utilization of built-in functions, these series of mini-cases were a well disposed - and yet great - way to review the essential knowledge of SQL.
